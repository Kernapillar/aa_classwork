Shakshuka recipe

Description

Shakshuka is an easy, healthy breakfast (or any time of day) recipe in Israel
and other parts of the Middle East and North Africa. It’s a simple
combination of simmering tomatoes, onions, garlic, spices and gently poached
eggs. It’s nourishing, filling and one recipe I guarantee you’ll make time and again.
